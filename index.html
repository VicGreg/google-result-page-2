speak

/spi:k/

verb

  1. say something in order to convey informqtion or to express a feeling.
    "in his agitation he was unable to speak"

    synonyms; talk, say
    (anything/something),utter,state, declare, tell, voice,
    express, prounce, articlate, enunciate, vocalize, verbalize, enounce

  2. (of behaviour, an object, etc.)
    serve as evidence for something.

    "his frame spoke tiredness"

    synonyms;indicate, mean, suggest, show, denote, display,
    demonstrate, be evidence of, register, reflect, reveal, betray,
    evince, disclose, exhibit, anifest, express, convey, signify, impart, bespeak,
    communicate, bear out, attest, testify to, prove, evidence, broken.

  3. (of a musical instrument or other object) make a sound when functioning.
    "the gun spoke again"
